I like books written after real event, but not only. Every book that teaches you something is a good one. Memorial journals are my favorites.

This is a list with my favorite books:

  • Amintiri din viata. 20 de ani in Siberia (Memories of life. 20 years in Siberia)

  • Zapada Insangerata (Blood Red Snow)

  • Tatuatorul de la Auschwitz (The tattooist of Auschwitz)

  • Baiatul cu Pijamale in dungi (The Boy in Striped Pajams)

  • Micul Print (The little Prince)

  • Maestrul si margareta (Master and Daisy)
